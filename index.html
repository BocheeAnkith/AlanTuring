About Alan Turing

Alan Turing, born in 1912, was a British mathematician whose ideas laid the groundwork for modern computing and artificial intelligence.

Legacy and Recognition

Turing's legacy lives on in fields from cryptography to AI. He is celebrated as a hero for his role in WWII and his pioneering ideas.

Projects and Innovations

Turing worked at Bletchley Park during WWII, where he played a crucial role in breaking the Enigma code, significantly impacting the Allied war effort.
